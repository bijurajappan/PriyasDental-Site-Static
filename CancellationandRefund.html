Cancellation & Refund Policy

Effective Date: 01-Sep-2025

At Dream Smile Dental Clinic, we value your time and strive to provide efficient services. Please read our cancellation and refund policy carefully.

1. Appointment Cancellation
  • Appointments may be rescheduled up to 24 hours in advance, subject to availability.
  • No refunds will be issued for cancellations, late arrivals, or no-shows.
  • If we must cancel or reschedule your appointment, we will notify you at the earliest and offer an alternative slot.
2. Refund Policy

For Online Dental Consultations:

  • No refunds under any circumstances.
  • These consultations are time-bound advice services, and once the slot is booked and paid for, the fee is non-refundable.

For In-Person (Dental OP) Services:

  • Consultation fees are non-refundable, as the payment is collected at the clinic itself prior to consultations.
  • Treatment fees are generally non-refundable. However, under specific and rare circumstances where:
    • A service was charged but not delivered due to reasons attributable to the clinic
  • You may submit a refund request in writing to drpriya.dreamsmile@gmail.com. We will review & refund the amount within 7-10 working days, to your mentioned Account# or GooglePay number or UPI ID, subject to approval
3. How to Request a Refund (For In-Person Only)

Email: drpriya.dreamsmile@gmail.com

Subject: Refund Request – [Your Name & Appointment Date]

Include:

  • Reason for refund request
  • Payment receipt
  • Account Number & IFSC Code OR Google Pay number OR UPI ID
  • Any supporting documents
